Legal Resources for Domestic Violence Survivors in New York State (NYS)

New York State Office for the Prevention of Domestic Violence (OPDV)

Website: https://opdv.ny.gov

The OPDV provides a range of resources, including information on domestic violence laws in New York, training programs, and services for survivors.

New York City Family Justice Centers (FJC)

Website: https://www1.nyc.gov/site/ocdv/programs/family-justice-centers.page

These centers offer comprehensive services, including legal assistance, to survivors of domestic violence, elder abuse, and more.

New York Legal Assistance Group (NYLAG):

Wesite: "https://nylag.org/

NYLAG provides free civil legal services to New Yorkers who cannot afford a private attorney, including those affected by domestic violence.

New York State Coalition Against Domestic Violence (NYSCADV)

Website: https://www.nyscadv.org/

NYSCADV is a statewide coalition that works to improve the response to and prevention of domestic violence. They can connect survivors to local resources.

Legal Aid Society of Northeastern New York

Website: https://www.lasnny.org/

LASNNY provides legal assistance and representation to low-income individuals and families in northeastern New York.

For legal assistance, please reach out to one of the above organizations or explore additional resources that may be available in your area.
